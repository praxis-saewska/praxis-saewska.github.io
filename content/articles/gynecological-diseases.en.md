Dear patients,

gynecological diseases such as uterine fibroids, endometriosis, ovarian cysts, and inflammatory processes are very common and can significantly affect quality of life. The good news is that with timely and professional diagnostics, most of these conditions respond well to therapy. My goal is not only to make a diagnosis, but to work together with you toward recovery.

I offer thorough examination, precise diagnosis, and gentle, individually adapted treatment. What matters to me is not just relieving your symptoms, but preserving your long-term health — with close attention to your personal needs and life circumstances.

## What to expect

### Uterine fibroids

- Individual assessment of fibroid size and location
- Conservative treatment (monitoring, medication) or surgical intervention
- Minimally invasive procedures (embolization, ablation)
- Complete surgical removal if necessary

### Endometriosis

- Diagnosis through ultrasound and clinical findings
- Hormonal therapy for pain relief
- Surgical removal of affected areas
- Support in pregnancy planning

### Ovarian cysts

- Ultrasound monitoring
- Distinction between functional and pathological cysts
- Conservative treatment or surgical removal
- Recurrence prevention

### Inflammatory diseases

- Diagnosis and treatment of adnexitis and pelvic inflammatory disease
- Antibiotic therapy
- Prevention of chronic complications

### Treatment methods

- Medical therapy
- Minimally invasive surgery (laparoscopy)
- Conventional surgery if necessary
- Supportive treatment and rehabilitation
