Dear patients,

as an experienced gynecologist, I understand how much bladder and pelvic floor problems can affect quality of life. Incontinence, pelvic floor weakness, and prolapse are medical conditions that can be successfully treated — and there is no reason to feel ashamed of them.

I am pleased to offer specialized urogynecology consultations with individual and comprehensive treatment. Together, we will find the optimal solution for you.

## What to expect

Individual examination, consultation, and treatment for the following conditions:

- Stress urinary incontinence (coughing, sneezing, physical exertion)
- Urge incontinence
- Mixed incontinence
- Pelvic organ prolapse
- Overactive bladder syndrome
- Chronic cystitis
- Pelvic pain

### Treatment methods

- **Conservative therapy** (medication, individualized recommendations)
- **Biofeedback** (specialized pelvic floor muscle training)
- **Botulinum toxin injections** (for overactive bladder)
- **Minimally invasive surgery** (tape/implant insertion)
- **Vaginal plastic procedures** (restoration of tissue and ligaments)
