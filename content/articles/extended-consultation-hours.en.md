## Important Announcement: New Consultation Hours

Dear Patients,

We are pleased to inform you that we will be extending our consultation hours starting **February 1, 2025**!

### The New Opening Hours

To offer you even more flexibility when scheduling appointments, we will be available for you at the following times starting in February:

- **Monday**: 08:00 - 18:00 (new: one hour longer!)
- **Tuesday**: 08:00 - 18:00
- **Wednesday**: 08:00 - 14:00
- **Thursday**: 08:00 - 19:00 (new: two hours longer!)
- **Friday**: 08:00 - 14:00

### What Does This Mean for You?

- More flexibility when choosing appointments
- Better compatibility with your work schedule
- Shorter waiting times with more available appointments

### Appointment Booking

You can book appointments as usual through our online booking system on Doctolib or by phone.

We look forward to serving you even better with these extended hours!

Your Praxis Saewska Team
