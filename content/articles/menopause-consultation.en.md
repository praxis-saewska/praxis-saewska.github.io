Dear patients,

menopause is not an ending but the beginning of a new phase of life full of possibilities. While it is often accompanied by unpleasant symptoms that can significantly affect quality of life, with the right support and treatment they can absolutely be managed. As a mother and gynecologist, I offer comprehensive counseling and individual treatment during this important time.

I firmly believe that every woman deserves to feel well — at every age. That is why I pay close attention not only to physical symptoms, but also to your overall well-being and quality of life.

## What to expect

Individual consultation and treatment in the following areas:

- **Assessment of menopausal symptoms** (hot flashes, night sweats, sleep disturbances, mood swings, dryness)
- **Hormone replacement therapy (HRT)** — individually adapted hormone therapy for symptom relief
- **Phytotherapy** — natural plant-based remedies (red clover, black cohosh, ginseng, etc.)
- **Lifestyle changes** — nutrition, physical activity, stress management
- **Osteoporosis prevention** — bone strengthening through vitamins and minerals
- **Cardiovascular health monitoring** after menopause

### Menopausal symptoms

We help with the following symptoms:

- Hot flashes and night sweats
- Sleep disorders and insomnia
- Mood swings and irritability
- Vaginal dryness and reduced libido
- Headaches and dizziness
- Weight gain and slowed metabolism
- Concentration problems
