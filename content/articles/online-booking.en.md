## Book your appointment online — anytime, with ease

Dear patients,

we are pleased to announce a convenient new feature: **you can now book your appointment with us online** — at any time of day or night, without waiting on the phone.

Online appointment booking is provided by our partner [arzt-direkt.de](https://arzt-direkt.de/).

### How to book your appointment

**In your browser:**

Visit our booking page and choose an available slot:

[→ Book an appointment](https://app.arzt-direkt.de/praxis-saewska/booking)

**Via app:**

For even more convenience, the arzt-direkt app is available for smartphones — free for Android and iPhone:

- [arzt-direkt on the App Store (iPhone)](https://apps.apple.com/de/app/arzt-direkt/id1155691979)
- [arzt-direkt on Google Play (Android)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.arztdirekt.patient)

### Your benefits

- Book appointments around the clock — even outside our opening hours
- View and manage your booked appointments
- Reminders via app notifications

### Note for pregnant patients

If you are pregnant, we kindly ask you to book your appointment **by phone** — this allows us to plan your visit in advance and ensure you receive the time and attention you need.

Of course, you are always welcome to reach us by phone for any other questions as well.

With kind regards,  
Dr. Saewska and the Praxis Dr. Saewska team
