Dear patients,

regular preventive examinations are the foundation of long and healthy longevity. Diseases detected at an early stage respond best to treatment and have the best prognosis.

I am pleased to offer comprehensive preventive examinations that help identify potential problems at an early stage. Together, we will create an individual health plan that is right for you.

## What to expect

### Examination components

- **General examination and consultation** — medical history, discussion of symptoms and risk factors
- **Gynecological examination** — assessment of the reproductive organs
- **Ultrasound (sonography)** — assessment of the uterus, ovaries, and breasts
- **Pap smear** — cervical cancer screening
- **Mammography or breast ultrasound** — breast cancer screening (for women over 40)
- **Laboratory tests** — blood tests for hormones, lipids, and glucose
- **Urine analysis** — kidney and urinary tract function assessment

### Recommended schedule

- Women under 30: annual examination
- Women aged 30–50: annual examination + breast ultrasound
- Women over 50: annual examination + mammography + laboratory tests

### Individual health plan

After the examination, we discuss together:

- Test and examination results
- Personal health risk factors
- Lifestyle recommendations (nutrition, physical activity, sleep)
- Plan for additional tests if necessary
- Schedule for the next examination
