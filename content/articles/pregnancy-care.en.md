Dear expectant mothers,

pregnancy is one of the most important periods in a woman's life. As a mother myself and an experienced gynecologist, I want you to experience this journey with joy and safety. I offer comprehensive pregnancy care with regular examinations, professional diagnostics, and individual counseling.

Every pregnancy is unique, and I approach each patient with particular attention and care. It is important to me that you feel well supported and can ask any questions you may have at every stage of your pregnancy.

## What to expect

- **Regular ultrasound examinations (sonography)** — monitoring fetal development, anatomy, and gestational age
- **Biochemical screening** — tests for chromosomal abnormalities (prenatal diagnostics)
- **CTG monitoring (cardiotocography)** — fetal heart rate and uterine contractions
- **Individual counseling** — all questions about pregnancy, birth, and breastfeeding
- **Blood pressure and weight monitoring** — maternal health control
- **Laboratory tests** — blood and urine analysis

### Consultation topics

- Nutrition and healthy lifestyle during pregnancy
- Physical activity and exercise
- Preparation for birth
- Pain relief during labor
- Breastfeeding
- Third trimester considerations
- Warning signs and when to see a doctor
