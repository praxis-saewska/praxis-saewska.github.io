## Renewed and ready — for you

Dear Patients,

It is our great pleasure to share some wonderful news: **the renovation of our practice is complete!** We thank you sincerely for your patience and understanding during these weeks — and we cannot wait to open our doors for you once again.

### What is new?

We made the most of this short break to make your visits even more comfortable, accurate, and relaxed:

- **Upgraded diagnostic equipment.** Modern devices allow us to examine more thoroughly and more efficiently — so we can take even better care of your health.
- **A fully digitalized practice.** Less paperwork, less waiting, more time for what truly matters — for you. Digital workflows make every appointment smoother.
- **A refreshed interior.** New walls and new details — yet the same warm, homely atmosphere where you can feel safe and cared for.

### Our doors reopen on May 5, 2026

Dr. Saewska and the entire team look forward to welcoming you back **starting May 5, 2026** — our long-time patients as well as anyone visiting us for the first time.

You can book your appointment as usual by phone.

We can hardly wait to see you again — in a renewed, yet just as familiar, practice!

Warmly,
Your Praxis Saewska Team
