Dear parents,

as a mother myself and an experienced gynecologist, I understand how important it is to take care of our children's health from an early age. I am therefore pleased to offer a specialized consultation in pediatric and adolescent gynecology, dedicated to the care of girls and teenagers up to the age of 18.

All examinations and consultations take place in a calm, friendly atmosphere. Every examination is explained in an age-appropriate way and discussed together with you and your daughter.

During the appointment, I will introduce your daughter to the basics of her physiology and puberty in a way that is easy to understand, provide guidance on proper self-care, and explain why it is important to pay attention to her own health.

## What to expect

Individual examination, consultation, and treatment in the following areas of pediatric gynecology:

- Early, delayed, or absent puberty
- Menstrual cycle disorders: irregular or absent periods
- Cycle disturbances following chemotherapy or radiation therapy
- Excessive hair growth, acne
- Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS)
- Congenital anomalies of the internal and external genitalia
- Genetic developmental disorders (Ullrich–Turner syndrome, Swyer syndrome, congenital adrenal hyperplasia, and others)
