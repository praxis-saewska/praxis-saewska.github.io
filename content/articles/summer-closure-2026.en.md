## Important Notice: Summer Holiday 2026

Dear Patients,

- Monday, 17.08.2026
- Wednesday, 19.08.2026

From **22.08.2026** onwards, we will be back to our regular opening hours as usual.

We sincerely thank you for your understanding and look forward to welcoming you back to our practice soon!
