## Our Services

### Pediatric and Adolescent Gynecology

A gentle introduction to women's health: We advise young girls and adolescents on all questions about the body, cycle, contraception, and first gynecological topics – in a trusting and respectful atmosphere.

### Urogynecology

A strong pelvic floor for better quality of life: We treat bladder and pelvic floor weaknesses, incontinence, or prolapse individually and holistically. Available treatments include medication, biofeedback, Botox treatments, minimally invasive surgeries (e.g., tape insertion), and vaginal plastic procedures.

### Pregnancy Care

Through pregnancy with joy and safety: We accompany you with regular ultrasound examinations (sonography), CTG monitoring, and individual counseling on all questions about pregnancy, birth, and breastfeeding.

### Menopause Consultation

A new phase of life with new possibilities: We support you during menopause with holistic counseling, hormonal or herbal therapy, and tips for your physical and emotional balance.

### Gynecological Diseases

We offer modern diagnostics and therapy for fibroids, endometriosis, ovarian cysts, and inflammatory diseases of the female reproductive organs. Our goal is gentle, individually adapted treatment – for your long-term health.

### Medical Abortion

In a protected and respectful setting, we offer counseling and performance of medical abortion according to legal requirements – with personal support and medical safety.

### Preventive Examinations

Regular preventive examinations are the best protection for your health. We perform comprehensive early detection examinations, smears, ultrasound, and individual health counseling.

### Treatment of Mastopathy

Sensitive care for benign breast changes: We clarify complaints, advise on self-examination, and accompany you medically competently through all phases of treatment.

### Vaccinations and Prevention

We advise on all important protective vaccinations – whether HPV, flu, pregnancy, or general women's health – and perform them directly in the practice.

### HPV Diagnostics and Prevention

We offer modern diagnostics, counseling, and treatment for HPV infections as well as comprehensive prevention to protect against cervical cancer.
