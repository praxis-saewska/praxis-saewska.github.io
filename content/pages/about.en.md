## Dr. PhD Elena Saewska

**Specialist in Gynecology and Obstetrics**

## Our Practice Philosophy

In our practice, individual care for each patient is the focus. We take time for your concerns and ensure a trusting, respectful atmosphere.

Our goal is to inform you comprehensively and find the best treatment option together with you. We combine modern medical standards with a holistic approach.

## What Matters to Us

- **Trust:** An open, trusting doctor-patient relationship is the basis of our work
- **Respect:** We treat every patient with respect and appreciation
- **Competence:** Regular training and modern treatment methods
- **Time:** We take time for your questions and concerns
- **Holistic Approach:** We consider not only the symptoms, but the entire health

## Training and Development

Medicine is a constantly evolving field. Therefore, I place great value on regular training and exchange with colleagues to always offer you the best possible treatment. I have regularly updated my qualifications through continuing education. Since my PhD in Surgery, I have authored more than 20 scientific publications and have extensive experience working with international teams in multidisciplinary clinics. During my time as Head of the Department of Operative Gynecology with Minimally Invasive Operations, I performed approximately 50 major and up to 30 minor procedures monthly, with 85% of operations being performed laparoscopically and hysteroscopically.
