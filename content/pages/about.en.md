## Qualifications and Education

- 1998-2004 - Medical degree, Crimean State Medical University 'S. Georgijevsky' (KGMU)
- 2004-2007 - Residency programs in General Surgery and in Obstetrics & Gynecology at Crimean Medical Institute 'S. Georgijevsky', Simferopol (one of the leading universities in Ukraine, Grade: 4.85)
- 2006-2009 - Postgraduate (PhD) studies in Surgery at KGMU, Simferopol
- 2009 - Defended PhD thesis (Candidate of Medical Sciences) at KGMU
- 2006-2011 - Clinical work in Endosurgery and Gynecology departments at N. Semashko University Clinic, Simferopol
- 2011-2018 - Faculty of Postgraduate Education at Crimean State Medical University. Lecturer in Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Simferopol
- 2011-2018 - Republican Clinical Hospital named after N. Semashko, Perinatal Center. Head of the Department of Gynecology with Minimally Invasive Surgery, Simferopol
- 2022 - Medical license (Approbation) in Germany (Landesamt für Gesundheit und Soziales, Berlin, ID: 2022-2194-BER)
- 2022 - Havelland Kliniken, Department of Gynecology and Obstetrics, Assistant Physician, Nauen and Rathenau
- 2024-2025 - Vivantes Klinikum Kaulsdorf, Continence and Pelvic Floor Center, Assistant Physician / Specialist, Berlin
- 2024 - Recognized by the Ärztekammer Berlin as Specialist in Gynecology and Obstetrics
- 2025-2026 - Vivantes Auguste-Viktoria-Klinikum, Clinic for Obstetrics, Specialist, Berlin

## Focus Areas and Specializations

- Minimally Invasive Gynecology (Laparoscopy and Hysteroscopy) in an ambulatory surgery setting
- Urogynecology — treatment of incontinence, correction of pelvic floor functional and anatomical disorders
- Pregnancy care, birth preparation and postpartum follow-up
- Pediatric and Adolescent Gynecology
- Comprehensive endometriosis management with individualized treatment planning
- Management of adnexal masses
- Treatment of uterine fibroids
- Assistance in selecting optimal contraception methods
- Comprehensive approach to age-related changes in women's health

## Our Practice Philosophy

In our practice, individual care for each patient is central. We take time for your concerns and provide an atmosphere of trust and respect.

Our goal is to inform you comprehensively and to find the best treatment option together. We combine modern medical standards with a holistic approach.

## What Matters to Us

- **Trust:** Open, trusting relationships between doctor and patient are the foundation of our work
- **Respect:** We treat every patient with respect and appreciation
- **Competence:** Regular continuing education and modern treatment methods
- **Time:** We take the time for your questions and concerns
- **Holistic Approach:** We consider not only symptoms but overall health

## Training and Development

Medicine is a constantly evolving field. Therefore, I place great value on regular training and exchange with colleagues to always offer you the best possible treatment. I have regularly updated my qualifications through continuing education. Since earning my PhD in Surgery I have authored more than 20 scientific publications and have extensive experience working with international teams in multidisciplinary clinics. While serving as Head of the Department of Operative Gynecology with Minimally Invasive Operations, I performed approximately 50 major and up to 30 minor procedures monthly, with 85% of operations performed laparoscopically and hysteroscopically.
